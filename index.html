About me

Welcome to my portfolio. I am Jaewoo An, but please just call me J. Born and raised in Seoul, I moved to the States for high school. I graduated from KAIST, one of the most prestigious engineering schools here in South Korea, with a bachelor's degree in bio & brain engineering. It covers a variety of academic fields, from anatomy and molecular biology to fluidics and dynamics to signal processing and circuit theory. But what really caught my attention was neuroscience and informatics. I was especially fascinated with neural networks. I worked my way through college, absorbing what I could from my professors and teaching myself what I felt was necessary. I started with data mining and optimization in C++. It took me awhile to learn the basics of computer science, but I passed the national data processer certificate examination with a very high score. Soon I was implementing machine learning models and statistical analysis in MATLAB and R. At this point, I was also teaching myself how to visualize data using igraph, Seaborn, and Gephi. And then it was deep learning in Python 3 using TensorFlow.

To this day, I am both excited and frustrated to know just how much knowledge is still out there for me to learn. I doubt the day will ever come when I finally get to call myself a true master. But there is one thing I know for certain: I will never stop learning. I am highly motivated to delve into whatever type of data I get my hands on, because I love what I do.
